Spacestation Game Ideas

Inspiration from games like Portal, Bioshock, Systemshock, Prey, The Witness, Outer Wilds

You come across a strange space station while travelling through space. You've never seen anything like it before. It seems to have appeared from out of nowhere

Game starts with you entering this strange space station. The place has been deserted. You are greeted by an AI speaking to you over the ships speakers

Similar to a metroidvania there will be items that you find in each area which will allow you to access to new areas and open up shortcuts between areas.

Game uses terminals and written notes to tell the story of the space stations passengers

Setting

A mysterious spacestation built for the purpose of scientific advancement. Each area of the spacestation has its own unique design and trials to overcome.
